my mission

After designing stickers on a small-scale, I recognized an opportunity to leverage my art for a broad social impact. I became inspired to develop a charitable program in which I design stickers for nonprofits and donate proceeds to these causes.

My multi-cultural background and diverse experiences shaped my identity and aspirations for this program. At a young age, I lived abroad in China and experienced difficulties of not speaking the language and fitting into mainstream groups. In addition, I value diversity and belongingness since my mother was born in South Africa and faced discrimination during Apartheid while my father endured war and poverty as a Vietnamese refugee.

Throughout school, I volunteered in soup kitchens and charity drives and created a club for disadvantaged elementary students. However, Serendipity Co provides me with a platform to implement change on a broader scale. Instead of helping 20 children at a time, I have managed to raise money and awareness among hundreds of people for Make-A-Wish, Save Yemen, and other organizations that support disadvantaged children.

Today, Serendipity Co supports six charities and serves over 3,000 customers across all 50 U.S. states and Europe.
