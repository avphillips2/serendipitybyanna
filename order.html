how to order


step one: choose your photo

photos can include people, pets, and other significant objects!


step two: choose your size

stickers can be anywhere from 1 - 4 inches.


step three: choose your finish

choose between either a glossy or matte finish.


step four: choose your quantity

quantity can be anywhere between 1 - 250 stickers.


step five: email me your preferences

message me on Etsy with your preferences for all of the above steps!

pricing

the prices below are the average payment for each type of sticker. however, other sticker details can be included and we can decide an appropriate price on a case-by-case scenario if you wish for additional items within the sticker.

price estimates:

1 person: $6.00 - $7.00
2-3 people: $7.50 - $8.50
